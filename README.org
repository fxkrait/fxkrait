Q: Will you ever become an [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Four_stages_of_awakening][arahant]]?

A: I worry that I've wasted and spent too much of my life on the carnal matters, and have lived too much of it in a undisciplined and unrestrained manner, to the point where I cannot attain such things at this point, given who I am and what I've become, my patterns and conditionings, and the time I have left. But we'll see what I can make happen here. The least I can do is try, and give it a noble effort. What choice do I have? Give up? That's not a choice anyone would sanely make. If Frank Yang got things really going by his 30's, I'll see what I can do. I may need to tweak the way I'm living, which is that part of the process I'm not too fond of, to be frank.
